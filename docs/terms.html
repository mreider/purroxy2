What Purroxy does

Purroxy is a desktop application that records browser interactions and replays them as automated capabilities. It connects to Claude Desktop via the Model Context Protocol (MCP) so that Claude can execute your saved automations. Purroxy runs locally on your computer; the backend API handles only accounts and subscriptions.

Your accounts and credentials

  • Purroxy accesses third-party websites using session cookies from your own authenticated sessions.
  • You are responsible for complying with the terms of service of any website you automate with Purroxy.
  • Purroxy never stores your passwords. It captures session cookies after you authenticate directly with the website.
  • Vault entries and session data are encrypted locally using your operating system's keychain. Kuvop LLC has no access to this data.
