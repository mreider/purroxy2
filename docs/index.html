How It Works

1

Log in once, securely

Enter a URL: your email, bank, insurance portal. You log in through a secure embedded browser. Credentials never touch any AI.

2

Record what you want done

Walk through the site. Every interaction is recorded as a reusable capability with parameters.

3

Ask Claude, it acts as you

Claude replays the recorded steps with your saved session, fills in search terms and filters, and extracts the results. Your password is never stored or shared.

Security is architecture, not policy

Other tools tell their AI "don't leak the password." Purroxy removes the password from the data before the AI ever sees it. Inputs and outputs are both protected. The difference matters.

Zero-knowledge login

You log in through the real website. Purroxy never sees your password, only the session cookies.

Encrypted vault

Credit cards, account numbers, and other sensitive data stored in your OS keychain. Typed into forms by Purroxy, never sent to Claude.

Data scrubbing

Vault values are removed from page content before Claude sees it. Not filtered by a prompt. Absent from the API call entirely.

Local extraction

At runtime, data is extracted directly from the page on your machine. No page content is sent to any AI. Sensitive results are redacted.

Automate your accounts with complete safety

Purroxy securely bridges your logged-in sessions to Claude Desktop. Your credentials never touch any AI. They stay encrypted on your machine.

// Claude searches your email for you
{
  "tool": "yahoo_mail__search_emails",
  "inputs": {
    "search_term": "tax documents"
  }
}
